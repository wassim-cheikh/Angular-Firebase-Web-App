What is Odoo ?

arrow_rightOdoo, formerly known as OpenERP is an open source ERP founded in 2004
that offers a full suite of fully integrated business management modules.

arrow_right Odoo is the most scalable and installed business management program
in the world thanks to its applications that meet all the needs of a company
from customer relationship management to the creation of websites and
e- trade, through production, inventory management, accounting, human
resources etc., all perfectly integrated. This is the first time that a software
publisher has managed to achieve such a level of functionality.
